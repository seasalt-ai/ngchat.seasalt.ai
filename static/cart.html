Key Features

Detailed Product Functionality
Store product data and real world knowledge in a generalized schema. Represent complex items such as combos, and products with variations or complex modifiers. Generate NLU training data from the menu.
Customizable Models
Answer complex queries and provide recommendations from the product list with Elastic Search. Offer suggestions for multi-item and combo orders with the Product Solver.
Natural Language Input & Output
Natural language in, natural language out. Integrate seamlessly with conversational AI by receiving natural language information from the user and flexibly selecting products with fuzzy and semantic matching.
